Description

An English Medium School & College for the civil and military human capital of Savar Area. Quality education for the children can empower pupils to be life long learners, improve quality of life and shape the destiny of the nation. With this understanding, Bangladesh Army came forward to fulfilling the need of the society and thus Morning Glory School & College (MGSC) came into being on August 28, 1999 in Savar Cantonment. Green campus, committed academic mentors, caring staffs and congenial teaching-learning environment are the uniqueness of this institution.
